Product description

It all fits. Wallet, keys, necessities, travel docs, & more … in one easy-to-carry crossbody bag. Six zippered pockets & elastic compartments & pleated bottom ensures everything fits.

Whether you're boarding a plane for an overseas jaunt or hopping in the car for a weekend getaway, one thing is certain: Staying organized is the key to a stress-free trip. No ordinary purse will do.

Your Soyater crossbody pocketbook features 6 zippered pockets to securely hold everything from your cell phone to your passport. The gusseted bottom of this medium bag expands to accommodate bulky must-haves such as books, tablets, and travel guides while elasticized side pockets are handy for snacks, keys, and more.

The price may be cheap, but your travel bag is built to last. It’s lightweight with a wide strap makes carrying your handbag more comfortable, even when you're out and about all day long. Plus, it's fully adjustable, so you can quickly change from a shoulder bag to a crossbody bag and back again. Not only that, but it's made from water-resistant nylon, so it protects your valuables and it's easy to clean, too.
